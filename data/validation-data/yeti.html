Product Overview

Frequently Asked Questions

PEOPLE ARE ASKING

See All FAQs
Is it dishwasher safe?

Yes. We recommend the base on the top rack, and gaskets in the utensil's basket. Of course, you can always hand wash, but we know you're busy on your next adventure.

Will the can cooler fit in my cupholders?

All can insulators are compatible with most cupholders. Also, here is your friendly reminder to never drink and drive any car, boat, four-wheeler, tractor, etc.

How do I use it?

To use the can and bottle cooler, remove the gasket and put the full can into the base then put the gasket back on. Do not pour your beverage into the base.

Does it only work with cold drinks?

These can insulators keep your drink cold on a hot day or keep it from freezing when you’re out in the cold.

I have an original Colster® Can Insulator model. What’s the difference?

The new gaskets will not fit the original Colster® Can Insulator models. We also added two new sizes for 16 oz. “tall boy” beers and slim cans like hard seltzers.

Can I get my Rambler® Drinkware customized?

Yes, Rambler® products can be customized but color exclusions may apply. Feel free to choose your favorite design from our gallery or customize your corporate order with your brand’s logo.

Is there a warranty on the can holder?

Rambler® Drinkware comes with a five-year warranty. For more information, please visit our Warranty Information page.
